--- GRADING POLICY ---
The final grade is calculated based on the following weight distribution:
- Weekly Quizzes: 20%
- Midterm Project: 30%
- Final Examination: 40%
- Class Participation: 10%

--- LATE WORK POLICY ---
Assignments submitted after the deadline will incur a penalty of 10% per day. 
Work submitted more than 5 days late will not be accepted and will receive a grade of zero. 
Exceptions are only made for documented medical emergencies or prior arrangements made at least 48 hours before the deadline.

--- TEXTBOOK AND MATERIALS ---
The primary textbook for this course is "Neural Networks: A Modern Approach" by Dr. Sarah Chen. 
Students are also required to have a subscription to the AI Research Lab platform for weekly coding assignments.

--- ATTENDANCE ---
Attendance is mandatory. Students are allowed two unexcused absences per semester. 
Each subsequent unexcused absence will result in a 2% deduction from the final participation grade.
